Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the strategy call really free?

Yes, completely free and with no obligation. We do a genuine audit of your practice's digital presence and present real recommendations — whether you work with us or not.

How long until I see results?

Websites and branding launch within 4–8 weeks. SEO typically shows meaningful growth in 3–6 months. Google Ads and social media campaigns can produce enquiries within the first week of launch.

Do you work with NHS or private practices?

Both — though we have extensive experience positioning mixed and fully private practices for premium patient acquisition. We tailor every strategy to your specific practice model.

Are there long-term contracts?

We operate on rolling monthly agreements after an initial 3-month onboarding period. Our 98% retention rate speaks for itself — clients stay because of results, not contracts.

Can you work with practices outside London?

Absolutely. We work with dental practices across the entire UK — from Edinburgh to Cornwall. Everything is handled remotely with regular video calls and dedicated account management.

What makes you different from other agencies?

We work exclusively with dental and aesthetic practices. This means deeper expertise, better results, and a team that speaks your language — from CQC compliance to treatment-specific patient journeys.
